# Barber's - Site Plan

## Business Overview

Barber's is a barber shop in the Orlando area,
they focus on delivering a high-quality spa-esque
treatment while people get their hair cuts.

**Target Audience:** People who get haircuts (any age)
and that want a spa type of treatment.

**Brand Personality:** Approachable, calming, luxurious.

## Page Structure

### 1. Home

- Hero section
- Featured cuts (3 images)
- Call-to-action to visit barbershop

### 2. About

- About section explaining why the brand exists

### 3. Services

- Page that details different services from regular haircuts
to eyebrow maintenence

### 4. Contact

- Shop location and hours
- Contact form
- Map placeholder
- Social Media links

## Design Direction

**Colors:**

- Background: Soft Linen (#F1E9DB)
- Primary: Rich Mahogany (#3C0000)
- Secondary: Burnt Tangerine (#D6431F)
- Terciary: Blue Slate (#5F7681)

**Typography:**

- Headings: Sans-Serif font (inviting, clean structured feel)
- Body: Clean sans-serif font (legible)

**Overall aesthetic:** Approachable, structured, clean. Lots of white space.
